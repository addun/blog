Smart Home refers to a residence equipped with internet-connected devices that automate and remotely control everyday functions
such as lighting, heating, security, and appliances.
These devices communicate over protocols like [Zigbee](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Zigbee), [Z-Wave](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Z-Wave), [Matter](https://csa-iot.org/all-solutions/matter/), or [Wi-Fi](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wi-Fi), and are often orchestrated by a central hub
or platform such as [Home Assistant](https://www.home-assistant.io/).
The goal is to improve comfort, energy efficiency, and convenience while keeping control of your data at home.
